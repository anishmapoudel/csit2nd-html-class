Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

Cooked Rice
Vegetables (Carrot, Peas, Capsicum)
Egg
Onion
Garlic
Soy Sauce
Salt and Pepper
Oil

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry onion and garlic.
  2. Add vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, salt and pepper.
  5. Stir fry everything together and serve hot.

Cooking Information

Information Details
Preparation Time 15 minutes
Cooking Time 20 minutes
